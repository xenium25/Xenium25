About XENIUM'25

XENIUM'25 is a visionary tech event hosted by the Department of Computer Applications at KE College Mannanam. It aims to bring together innovation, creativity, and expertise in an electrifying atmosphere of learning competition.

Penalty Shootout

1.5K

Prize Pool

₹100

Reg Fee

Registration Starts at 9:00 AM!

Game Rules

  • Each team must consist of 3 players: 3 on-field players.
  • The event follows a knockout format.
  • Each team will have 4 penalty kicks per round.
  • In case of a tie, a tie-breaker round with one kick per team is conducted.
  • If the tie-breaker also ends in a tie, a toss will be conducted.
  • The team that wins the toss has two options:
    • Kick First: Score to qualify; fail to score results in elimination.
    • Save First: Prevent the opponent's kick to qualify; fail to save results in elimination.
  • Teams failing to appear on time for their kicks will forfeit their match, and a walkover will be given to the opposing team.
  • All players must bring their own boots for the game.
  • All decisions made by the organizing committee are final and binding.
  • Only the three teams that register first can participate in the event from K E College.
